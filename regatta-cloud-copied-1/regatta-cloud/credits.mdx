title: "Credits"

The **Credits** page presents a detailed, read‑only view of all credits associated with the account, including active, expired, and fully used credits. This page allows users to track credit allocation, usage, validity periods, and consumption progress over time. It makes it easy to monitor consumption patterns, identify expired allocations, and understand remaining available credits at a glance.

At the top of the page, a **search field** labeled _“Search credits…”_ enables filtering credit entries. As with other pages, filtering applies across all visible fields in the table, and column header arrows allow sorting behavior identical to the Clusters and Backup & Restore pages.

A **refresh button** on the right reloads the credits data to ensure the most up‑to‑date usage and status information is displayed.

## Credits Table

The table lists individual credit entries. Each row represents a single credit allocation period.

The table includes the following columns:

- **Type**\
  Indicates the source or category of the credits, such as _INITIAL_ or _BONUS_.
- **Start Date**\
  The date from which the credits become valid.
- **End Date**\
  The expiration date of the credit allocation.
- **Status**\
  Displays the current state of the credits with a visual indicator:
  - **ACTIVE** – credits are currently valid and can be used
  - **EXPIRED** – the credit validity period has ended
  - **USED** – the entire credit amount has been consumed up until now
- **Used**\
  Shows the monetary value of credits that have already been consumed during the validity period.
- **Total**\
  Displays the total amount of credits allocated for that entry.
- **Usage Indicator**\
  A visual progress bar combined with a percentage value indicates how much of the total credit allocation has been used. For example, partially filled bars represent partial usage, while a full bar indicates 100% consumption.
