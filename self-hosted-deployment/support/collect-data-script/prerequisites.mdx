---
title: Prerequisites
---

To use the script, you will require the following:

1. From Regatta support:

    a. The collect_data script

    b. The storage bucket particulars for data upload

    c. The service_account_json_file to access the bucket
2. Dependencies:

    a. google-cloud-storage version 2.0.0 and above

    b. zstandard version 0.20.0 and above

    c. python3 version between 3.6.8 and 3.8.x
    
3. Outbound Internet connection to upload the collected data

<Note> Note that some prerequisites will be optional depending on the manner you decide to collect 
the data. </Note>
