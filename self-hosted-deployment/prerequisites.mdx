---
title: Prerequisites
---

The following should be available
- Servers to deploy Regatta Cluster on
     - OS: Oracle 8 or REHEL 8.10
     - Required dependencies:
        - shadow-utils
        - glibc 
        - openssl-libs
        - libaio
        - ncurses-compat-libs
        - python3-pip
    - Memory: At least 32 GB
    - Disk space for deployment: 300 MB per software module
    - CPUs: At least 4 CPUs
    - Operational storage devices: At least one 350 GB NVMe or SSD, which is not the 
    system device, which can be dedicated to the Regatta cluster

- Connectivity
    - At least 10GB network between all Regatta servers

- Software
    - Download all RPMS from Regatta and have them readily available to distribute to
the servers.
